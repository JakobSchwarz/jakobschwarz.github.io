Our Company

Our team is composed of professional engineers, programmers, designers, engineering technologists, and experienced journeymen who are constantly working on the latest technologies in Building Automation, CCTV Systems, Card Access Systems, Security Systems, and Fire Alarm Systems, designing, building, and servicing customers all across the province and Western Canada. With over 115 years of combined experience, SISU is capable of working on the most complex designs to produce the most optimized solutions for an ever-evolving market.
